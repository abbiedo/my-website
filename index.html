About This Site

This site is a resource for topics in traditional Jewish literature and liturgy, especially piyyutim, or liturgical poems.

My name is Abbie Denemark Ossip. I enjoy studying Jewish liturgy, especially piyyutim. One of my favorite things about piyyut (and other Jewish writings) is the way the writers weave in (and occasionally subvert) verses from Tanach, whether in direct quotations or references.

I hope you enjoy the resources on this site. I intend to upload new ones as I make or find them. Contact me if you have any questions or would like to see your favorite piyyut translated and posted.
